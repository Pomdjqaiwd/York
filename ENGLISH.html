City Description

Explore the vibrant neighborhoods, world-class museums, and renowned restaurants that make NYC one of the most exciting cities in the world.

Dangerous Areas

While New York City is generally a safe place to visit, there are some areas that may be more dangerous than others. It's important to be aware of your surroundings and take precautions when exploring the city.

  • The area around Longwood Avenue in the Bronx - there are 25 crimes per thousand residents here.
  • Harlem in Manhattan - a center of unemployment and crime. It's better not to be here in the evening and at night.
